Ambā pregnancy tracker

UI/UX
Pregnancy can be very confusing and tough on mental health, with scattered unstructured articles all over the internet, information overload and misinformation are real threats. Ambā was designed as a weekend exercise to solve the aforementioned problem. With all the relevant information handy on the homepage, and reliable and verified articles on the feed section, pregnancy can be a little bit easier.
