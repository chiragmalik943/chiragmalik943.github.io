Starclinch Onboarding

UI/UX
Starclinch is a mobile application that helps users find and book services from local professionals. The app allows users to search for services by category, location, or keyword, and provides a list of the best professionals available. The app also includes a feature to book services, track the progress of the service, and provide feedback on the service. The app is designed to be user-friendly and easy to use, with a clean and modern interface. The app is available for both iOS and Android devices.
