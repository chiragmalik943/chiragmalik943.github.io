ISRO Space Station

3D Modeling
The ISRO Space Station is a 3D model of the Indian Space Research Organisation's (ISRO) space station. The model was created using Blender, a free and open-source 3D modeling software. The model includes various components of the space station, such as the main module, the service module, and the solar arrays. The model was created to provide a visual representation of the space station and to help visualize the design and layout of the station.
